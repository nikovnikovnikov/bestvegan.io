Hanoi, the northern capital of Vietnam, is over 1000 years old. Its winding alleys, ancient architecture, and frenetic energy will leave you dizzy in love. The colorful southeast Asia megacity is home to Plaza 99 and dozens of other malls catering to people from all walks of life.
